Information

The NMMS (National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship) exam is for students in Class 8 from economically weaker sections. It aims to provide financial assistance for continued education. Eligibility: Class: Must be in Class 8. Income Limit: Family income ≤ ₹1.5 lakh/year. Performance: Minimum 55% in Class 7 (50% for SC/ST). Exam Structure: Mental Ability Test (MAT): Assesses reasoning skills. Scholastic Aptitude Test (SAT): Covers subjects like Maths, Science, and Social Science. Scholarship: Amount: ₹500–₹1,200 per month (varies by state). Application: Through the state education board, with necessary documents.
